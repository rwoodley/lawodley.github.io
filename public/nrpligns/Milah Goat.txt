As if mountaineering wasn't hard enough, now the mountain goats themselves are a challenge. The milah goat, named after the mountain range it resides in, comes in at around 10 feet tall. Its nimble limbs and hooked claws allow it to scale vertical cliffsides in seconds, and its thick wooly coat keeps it warm in the thickest blizzards. Yes, they are carnivorous, and no, they aren't picky. The best defense is to simply not hike in their territories.
