Large, flightless birds that inhabit forests of wildflowers. They are born without feathers, but their skin exerts a gooey substance which provides the necessary nutrients for seeds to take root. Over the course of their lives, Flowts cover their bodies in beautiful displays of flowers to camouflage and attract mates. Since their flower outfits ("floutfits") come from their surroundings, the region a Flowt is from can be determined by the growth on its body!
