A vicious beast of thunder and lightning, the bolttail is a bird of prey which lives inside thunderstorms and hunts unsuspecting prey that wanders inside. Their tails are made of pure electricity, which they use as stingers to shock and subdue their enemies. Bolttails are susceptible to electromagnetic radiation, specifically radio, and stay far from cities to avoid the electrical interference. In an increasingly globalized world, there are few remaining refuges where bolttails can escape the constant radio chatter of a growing civilization.
