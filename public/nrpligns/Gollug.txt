Many cultures view the Gollug as the protector of the forest. The beasts spend most of their life deep underground. People say they only surface when an outside force poses a threat to the woods in which they live. Such legends have long since been ruled out by science, but the reason as to why Gollugs emerge from the ground is still unknown. Two things are for certain: they are undeniably strong, and discriminately aggressive. If you are in the presence of a Gollug, it's best to run and hide before you meet a swift end by its crystal blade.
