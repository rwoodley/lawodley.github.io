Why be strong when you can just be terrifying. This is the philosophy the smogskrene lives by. Using its internal fire (literally) and the spout atop its head, the smogskrene shapes and manipulates smoke so that it takes the form of a large, aggressive monster. However, this is all an illusion. The smoke is not alive, and is no stronger than a gentle spring breeze. Luckily for the smogskrene, its predators have yet to figure this out.
