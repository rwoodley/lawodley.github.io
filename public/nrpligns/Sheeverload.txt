Some animals make you question how they made it this far, but none will have you as baffled as the sheeverload. Found in the rocky, savanna mesas, these bug-eyed beasts are exceptionally bad at… well, living. They eat their own young, unable to distinguish between them and their real food. Their bones are brittle, especially so for the harsh environment they find themselves in. Against all odds, the sheeverload continues to reproduce and survive, even when all odds say this thing should have died out ages ago.
