Small, carnivorous, narwhal-like animals that hunt in packs. Dromeostodons swim beneath iceflows, searching for prey that wanders out onto the ice. In coordinated attacks, they jab upwards through the ice with their spiral horn, sinking their victims into icy water before consuming them in a feeding frenzy. In the past, they were hunted to the brink of extinction for their ivory horns, but the species has made an amazing comeback due to new protections.
