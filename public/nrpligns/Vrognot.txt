Most boulders on the forest floor are just boulders, but a select few are actually vrognots. The vrognot burrows into the ground until its head and limbs are completely hidden, leaving only its rocky cranium exposed. There it lies, letting life pass it by as it slumbers in the cool dirt. Why they do this, we aren't yet sure. It seems like simply a defense mechanism, although they spend a ludicrous amount of time just… sitting there. Maybe the key to camouflaging as a rock is just acting like a rock.
